# Programs & Facilities (MCC East Bay)

## What community programs does MCC offer?
MCC runs a wide variety of programs including after-school Qur’an classes, Sunday school, weekly halaqas, youth groups, and more. :contentReference[oaicite:16]{index=16}

## Can I rent MCC facilities?
MCC provides a facility rental form and details on the Forms & Docs page for community use.

## Are there support services like food pantry or refugee support?
Yes — MCC’s community services include food pantry, refugee support, and other assistance programs.

## What if I want to suggest a program or speaker?
MCC accepts suggestions for community programs directly via the website or events contacts.
