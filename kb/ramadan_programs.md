# Ramadan Programs (MCC East Bay)

## Are there special youth programs during Ramadan?
MCC hosts youth halaqas with Asr prayer followed by Iftar and activities for middle & high school teens during Ramadan. :contentReference[oaicite:9]{index=9}

## Does MCC have community Iftars?
MCC organizes Community Iftar events that require registration, often featured on the MCC events calendar. 

## Are there special needs Iftar programs?
MCC holds special needs Iftar events with tailored accommodations, including accessible seating and sign language interpretation.
