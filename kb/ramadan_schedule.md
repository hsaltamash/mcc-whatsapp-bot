# Ramadan Schedule (MCC East Bay)

## How does MCC announce the start of Ramadan?
MCC follows confirmed global crescent moon sightings and announces the beginning of Ramadan via its newsletter and official channels.

## What happens during the moon-sighting event?
MCC organizes a skygazing event where the community looks for the new crescent moon; food trucks and viewing telescopes are often present.

## When does nightly Taraweeh occur?
Nightly Taraweeh prayers are held indoors after Isha throughout Ramadan; times and specific recitation schedules are announced close to Ramadan.
