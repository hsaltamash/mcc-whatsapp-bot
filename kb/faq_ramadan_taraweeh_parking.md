# Ramadan & Taraweeh (MCC East Bay)

## When does taraweeh start?
Taraweeh starts after Isha. MCC announces taraweeh start details on its Ramadan events/calendar and communications.

## Where do I park for taraweeh and iftars?
For Jumu’ah, iftars, and taraweeh, MCC notes that worshippers may park in the neighboring Hewlett Packard lot (now the Pleasanton School District lot), with MCC parking security helping direct motorists.

## Will there be parking/security support during taraweeh?
MCC states the parking security team stands watch during taraweeh and directs motorists.

## How does MCC confirm the start of Ramadan?
MCC describes following confirmed crescent moon sightings to determine the start of Ramadan, and states it will confirm the start of Ramadan in its newsletter and announcements.

## How will I know the official “first night of taraweeh” and first day of fasting?
MCC’s moonsighting guidance explains scenarios depending on whether the moon is sighted; MCC then confirms via its announcements/newsletter.
