# Kids & Families (MCC East Bay)

## Does MCC have activities for kids?
MCC hosts programs such as toddler playgroups, Sunday school, and storytime for children as part of community services. 

## Are youth halaqas and programs offered?
Yes — youth halaqa and activities including games and discussions are offered regularly, especially during special community events. 

## Is there childcare at events like Special Needs Iftar?
Special needs friendly events often include accommodations and support for children and caregivers. 

## Does MCC include families in Ramadan events?
Yes — many programs like community iftars and moon-sighting events are designed for the whole family.
