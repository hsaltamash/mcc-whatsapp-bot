# Zakat & Donations (MCC East Bay)

## How do I apply for zakat assistance through MCC?
MCC has a zakat application process (including an online/printable application) and explains that the committee may review and contact applicants/references as part of due diligence.

## How long does zakat processing take?
MCC notes that processing time can be up to seven business days (depending on application volume).

## Who do I contact for zakat questions?
MCC lists zakat-committee@mcceastbay.org and a phone contact for the zakat administrator.

## What donation methods does MCC accept (and not accept)?
MCC lists multiple ways to give (check, employer matching, stock/wire/bequests, DAFs, etc.) and notes it does not accept Zelle, Venmo, PayPal, or other donation platforms (per the Ways to Give page).

## Where do I mail a check donation?
MCC provides a mailing address for check donations.
