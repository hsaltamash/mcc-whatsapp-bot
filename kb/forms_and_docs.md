# Forms & Documents (MCC East Bay)

## Where can I find MCC forms (zakat application, membership, facility rental, etc.)?
MCC maintains a Forms & Docs page that links to common forms such as zakat application, membership, facility use/rental, and more.

## How do I access the zakat application form?
The Forms & Docs page includes a zakat application link (online and/or downloadable).

## Who do I contact if I have questions about forms?
MCC lists an operations email contact on the Forms & Docs page.
