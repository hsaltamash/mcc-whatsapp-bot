# Prayer Times & Services (MCC East Bay)

## Where can I see today's prayer times (adhan and iqamah)?
MCC posts today’s prayer times on the MCC homepage and on the Prayer page, including both adhan and iqamah times. 

## What time is Isha at MCC?
Isha times are published daily on MCC’s prayer times page (adhan and iqamah). Check MCC “Prayer” / homepage for today’s exact Isha time.

## What time is Jumu’ah on Friday?
MCC lists multiple weekly Friday Jumu’ah services (including times at MCC and an offsite Rosewood service). Check the Prayer page for the current Friday schedule.

## The 1:30 pm Jumu’ah is crowded—what should I do?
MCC notes heavy parking congestion for the popular 1:30 pm service and recommends attending the Rosewood option or other less congested services when possible.

## How does MCC determine prayer times?
MCC publishes prayer time guidelines describing how prayer times are determined, including methodology details and general principles used by the MCC Religious Committee.
