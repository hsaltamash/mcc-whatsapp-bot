# Friday Jumu’ah (Parking & Offsite Option)

## Why does MCC have an offsite Jumu’ah(Juma) option at Rosewood?
MCC offers Juma operation inside its main location 5724 W Las Positas Blvd #300, Pleasanton, CA 94588. It also offers a year-round offsite Jumu’ah option to ease parking congestion at the main facility.

## What time is the Rosewood Jumu’ah (Juma)?
MCC describes the weekly Rosewood service timing window and encourages it as an option when the popular MCC service is congested.

## What is the address of Rosewood Jumu'ah(Juma) prayer location?
The address of Rosewood Jumu'ah prayer location is 4450 Rosewood Dr, Pleasanton, CA 94588

## Parking note: can I park in neighboring lots around MCC?
MCC explicitly cautions not to park in neighboring lots around MCC when permission isn’t granted, and suggests attending a less congested service instead.
